Who I Am

About Me

I am Dilay Selim, a computer engineering student with a growing focus on iOS development, web design, and user-centered digital experiences. I enjoy turning ideas into polished interfaces that feel clear, modern, and easy to use.

My current interests include semantic HTML, responsive CSS, accessibility, clean component structure, and building products that blend technical logic with visual storytelling. I also work with Xcode and Swift as I continue shaping my path as a developer.

“A good website should be beautiful, functional, and accessible to everyone.”

— A principle that guides my work
Read more about me

I love creating projects that feel intentional from top to bottom — from the structure of the HTML to the smallest hover effect. I pay attention to hierarchy, readability, and usability because strong design is not only about appearance, but also about how confidently people can move through a website.

Beyond coding, I am motivated by continuous growth. Every project helps me improve my problem-solving, design sense, and development workflow. My goal is to keep building experiences that are both technically strong and visually memorable.
